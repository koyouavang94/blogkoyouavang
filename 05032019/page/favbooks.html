i thank the universe for taking everything it has taken and giving to me everything it is giving. rupi kaur.

Milk and Honey helped me grow as an individual, as a sister, as a daughter, and as a girlfriend. It made me more aware of my surroundings, my actions, and my love for myself and everyone. It brought out a deeper, more intimate and vulnerable part of myself that I was scared to touch. Now, when I read this book it reminds me of my journey: where I was, where I began, and where I am now.
